❤️

Reading the HR-based numbers: all zones and thresholds here key off heart rate, which drifts with heat, dehydration, fatigue, poor sleep, caffeine and altitude — and rises late in long climbs (cardiac drift) even at steady effort. Treat HR caps as a guide, not a hard ceiling: on a hot day or a big mountain stage the same effort will show a higher HR, so go by perceived effort and breathing too. The estimated W/kg above is a coarse VO2max-derived proxy, not a measured power figure — a trend line, not a target.
