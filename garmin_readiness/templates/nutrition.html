12-Week Training Plan

Nutrition

Asda protein pot + shake · Uncle Bens rice pouches · Gousto dinners · 160 g+ protein daily

Protein first

160–180 g daily protects muscle over 50. Asda protein pot + shake alone gives 55 g before lunch. Add rotisserie chicken or tuna to every rice pouch.

Carbs around rides

Add oats + banana to the protein shake breakfast on training days. Uncle Bens pouches already deliver 65–70 g carbs at lunch — use them deliberately.

Zero prep

Protein pot + shake: open and drink. Uncle Bens: 2 min microwave. Rotisserie chicken: pull off the breast. Nothing to cook or refrigerate.

Gousto dinners

Gousto provides structure, portion control, and variety. Use calorie bands as a guide when choosing weekly recipes.

Alternate the pouches

Switch between Egg Fried Rice (~380 kcal) and Paella (~350 kcal) to keep lunch interesting. Both pair equally well with chicken or tuna.

Long ride: fuel up

Sunday long rides need the biggest oats + banana breakfast, on-bike carbs every 45 min, then the Uncle Bens + chicken + tuna recovery pouch immediately after.

On-bike nutrition

45–60 min rides

  • Before: Banana 30–45 min prior
  • During: Water only — no food needed
  • After: Asda chocolate milk 300 ml within 20 min

60–90 min rides

  • Before: Full breakfast 90 min prior
  • During: 1 banana at 45 min (~23 g carbs)
  • After: Asda chocolate milk immediately

90 min–3 hrs

  • Before: Big oats breakfast 2–2.5 hrs prior
  • During: 40 g carbs every 45 min
  • After: Chocolate milk + Uncle Bens recovery pouch
